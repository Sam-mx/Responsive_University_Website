Faculty of Science and Engineering

The Faculty of Science and Engineering at the University of Sam (UOS) is at the forefront of scientific education, research, and innovation.

We provide access to state-of-the-art laboratories, research facilities, and equipment, enabling students and researchers to conduct experiments and investigations that drive scientific progress.

Faculty of Health

The Faculty of Health at the University of Sam (UOS) is dedicated to preparing the next generation of healthcare professionals, researchers, and leaders.

UOS provides access to modern laboratories and clinical training facilities,which offer students hands-on experience and the opportunity to practice their skills in real-world healthcare settings.

Faculty of Business

The Faculty of Business is dedicated to equipping students with the knowledge and skills to excel in the dynamic world of business and entrepreneurship.

We have close ties to the business world and provide students with opportunities to interact with professionals in the field, intern at prestigious firms, and take part in actual business initiatives.
