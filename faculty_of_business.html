Our strategic principles

Our work is underpinned by six strategic principles.

Inclusion

Be inclusive and encourage diversity in all of our activities

RESPONSIBLE LEADERSHIP

Initiative discussion and be a trusted thought leader on the role of business in society

INNOVATION

Bring new ideas and evidence-based expertise to bear on critical issues of public interest and value

PUBLIC GOOD

Lead education and research that enables business and organsations to contribute to the public good

EXCELLENCE

Deliver excellence in business teaching, research, community and industry engagements

EQUALITY

Use education and research as a pathway to individual mobility, social diversity and economic equality
