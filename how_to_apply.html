Learn how to apply to UOS

University of Sam

No matter the path you’ve taken to get to this point, here are the next steps on your journey to studying here.

  • Choose your course: Discover your ideal program that aligns with your goals—single or double degree, tailor your educational journey with us.
  • Explore your entry pathways: Explore diverse entry pathways, from standard admissions to transfers or international exchanges. Choose the path that aligns with your background and aspirations.
  • See entry requirements: Understand entry requirements for your program, including academic criteria and necessary documentation, ensuring a seamless application process.
  • Apply: After selecting your course, exploring pathways, and reviewing requirements, apply online. Ensure all necessary documents are included; our admissions team is here to support you.

If you need to contact us, please provide your Universities Admissions Centre (UAC) application ID number and your direct eApplication ID number.
