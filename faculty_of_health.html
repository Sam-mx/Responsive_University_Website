RESEARCH INSTITUTE FOR INNOVATIVE SOLUTIONS FOR WELL-BEING AND HEALTH

OUR VISION
The newly developed Research Institute for Innovative Solutions for Well-being and Health (INSIGHT) at the University of Technology Sydney will transform health outcomes in our community by improving clinical care and health, driving responsive health policy, informing sustainable models of health care and health systems, and building the health care and health research workforce of the future.

INSIGHT

Who we are

The UOS Faculty of Health is a broad and multi-disciplinary faculty that addresses health from conception to end of life.

The Faculty of Health is a dynamic and growing faculty delivering outstanding results. We are recognised both nationally and internationally for the excellence in our teaching and the quality and impact of our research.

A long-standing partner of health professions, the faculty is highly engaged with the health sector, industry, practitioners, and the worldwide research community.

Who we are

Our impact

With social justice at our core, the work of INSIGHT will drive our commitment to address health inequity in line with the sustainable development goals.

Our research takes a person-centred, life-course approach underpinned by the responsible use of technology that harnesses data-based decision making. These values stand out in our strong research translation track record, where we deliver solutions that are locally and globally meaningful, making a real difference to people’s lives.
