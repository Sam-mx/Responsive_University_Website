UOS's Library


Our library facilities are designed to support your academic success and provide a comfortable environment for research and study. Here are some of the outstanding facilities you'll find at the UOS Campus library:

1. Extensive Collection

Our library houses a vast collection of books, journals, e-books, and multimedia resources, covering a wide range of academic disciplines. Whether you're studying engineering, literature, or biology, you'll find valuable materials to support your coursework and research.

2. Quiet Study Areas

For focused and uninterrupted study, we offer quiet study areas with individual carrels and group study rooms. These spaces are designed to help you concentrate and collaborate effectively.

3. Digital Resources

We provide access to a rich array of digital resources, including online databases, academic journals, and e-books. These resources are accessible on-campus and remotely, giving you the flexibility to conduct research from anywhere.

4. Technology and Computing

Our library is equipped with computer workstations, printers, and scanners. You can also borrow laptops and other devices to aid your research and complete assignments. Our tech-savvy staff is available to assist with any technology-related inquiries.

5. Expert Assistance

Our team of expert librarians is dedicated to helping you make the most of our resources. They can assist with research guidance, citation support, and information literacy skills. Don't hesitate to seek their guidance during your academic journey.


Experience the convenience and excellence of our library facilities at UOS Campus. Your academic success is our priority.

Campus cafés and restaurants


Our campus is not just a place for academic growth but also a hub for relaxation and socialization. Explore the fantastic café, bar, and restaurant facilities at UOS Campus:

Café: Energize Your Day

Start your day with a freshly brewed cup of coffee or choose from a selection of teas and snacks at our café. It's the perfect spot for a quick recharge between classes or a cozy spot to study with friends.

Bar: Unwind and Connect

Our campus bar is the ideal place to unwind after a long day. Enjoy a variety of beverages, including soft drinks and alcoholic options, in a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere. It's a great venue to meet fellow students and make lasting connections.

Restaurant: Savor Delicious Meals

Whether you're looking for a quick lunch or a full dining experience, our campus restaurant has you covered. With a diverse menu that caters to various dietary preferences, you can savor delicious meals with friends and classmates.

Outdoor Seating

On sunny days, you can take advantage of our outdoor seating areas. Enjoy your meals and beverages in the fresh air while appreciating the campus's green spaces and vibrant atmosphere.



At UOS Campus, we believe in nurturing not only your academic pursuits but also your social well-being. Join us in our café, bar, and restaurant facilities for a fulfilling campus experience!
